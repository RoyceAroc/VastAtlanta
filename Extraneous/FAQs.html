Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account?

An account, using Google of Facebook, is not necessary for you to complete buying the product. It is only used in means of user friendliness to help save orders, bring you some rewards, and for you to get notifications about nearby deals and discounts.

How long does arriving shipment take?

Delivery takes about 2-3 days based upon your location from our headquarters in Cumming, GA. We only ship In-State , and if you're Out-Of-State you would have to come by and grab your product. If a product is purchased after Thursday at 5pm, expect your product to come later - since we are closed off in the weekend. You can also view your Delivery Info at our site as needed.

What is the difference between Dry and Wet Slides?

Ironically enough, Dry Slides do not have water flowing, whereas Wet Slide do. Hence the name, this should not change your in mind in the decision making process.

Where may I discuss information freely with other people, regarding Vast Atlanta?

For this sole purpose, our developers have created a Forum. You must be signed in to enter the forum. Upon signing in, click on ☰, at the top-right corner. Select Forum.

Do Concession Machines come loaded with goodies?

Unfortunately, Concession Machines don't come with your favorite candy and snacks. Instead, we do provide money, labels, a key, and a charger :)

What happens if I break my product, such as a Chair or Table?

100% no charge!!! Many of our customers are proud of of this deal, and we are a 100% kind towards accidents.

If all of this is a rental, how does the foam and soap part work?

That's a good question! You, as the customer, get to use all of the fluid and liquid inside the bottle, but the container is meant to be returned back to us. Why so? Well, we melt this plastic to create other products: whereby procuring biodiversity.

Once my rental time runs out, where do I give back my product?

Once you have had a fun time, hopefully ;), you have two options to return the product. If your payment was over $50, then we provide a truck that comes to get back our product. If your payment is below $50, please return the product back to the store!

Oh no, I forgot to give the product back...days after the rental period...

Don't panic! I bet you did, when you opened this register! You have an extended rental period of 2 days (we are 100% kind folks). Sadly, if you go ver 2 days, you have to pay us double the rate of the product as each day passes :(
